SoppyCraft – Indoor Plants Pixel Pack (Free Version) 🪴
========================================================

This is a free sample from the full Indoor Plants & Display Cabinet pack — a cozy pixel art collection for decorating interiors, botanical shops, or inventory screens in your games.

🌱 Try it, use it, and if you love it, upgrade to the full version for even more variety and charm!

-------------------

├── Aseprite/
│   ├── sc_indoor_plants.aseprite        ← All included plants
│   ├── sc_indoor_plants_w_pot.aseprite  ← Combined with base pot
